Persoanl Profile

Bringing people together. Providing talent. Supporting teams. Creating great things. In my life as a solutions architect, business analyst, system analyst, project manager, program manager I came to a realisation that in the fast moving world, a frequent assessment and adaptation of plans is required to deliver usable and fit for purpose components, and that’s how I got interested in agile software development – especially in Scrum. With this new-found knowledge, I started supporting my team by allowing them to find their own innovative rhythm and know that not one size fits all. It’s always an exciting experience to see how teams can outgrow themselves. I love sharing my passion with others. If I choose a project, I’m all in, and my motivation easily infects others to achieve a common goal. The method is second place, but needs to be a good fit with the team. Next to Scrum, Kanban and attention to detail also provide amazing results. When someone chooses to work with me, they are bringing a strong contender to the table – and ensuring to synergise for continuous improvement. I see it as my job to move the team forward and to protect it from negative influences. In the process, things can get uncomfortable, but with a lot of perseverance, the team, management, always come together as one, find effective compromises, and deliver excellent results. I make things happen!!!
